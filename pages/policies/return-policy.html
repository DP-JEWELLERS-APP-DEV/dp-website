Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to be absolutely delighted with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a transparent and flexible return and exchange policy.

15-Day Return Policy

Unused and unworn jewellery in its original condition can be returned within 15 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange. The product must be accompanied by the original certificate, invoice, and packaging.

Lifetime Exchange

We offer lifetime exchange on all our jewellery. If you wish to upgrade or exchange your DP Jewellers piece, we will provide the prevailing market rate for the gold and precious stones, subject to our quality check.

Conditions for Return

  • The product should not show any signs of wear and tear or resizing by another jeweller.
  • Custom-made or personalized jewellery cannot be returned.
  • The item must be in its original packaging.
